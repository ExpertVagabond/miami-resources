# Miami Resources
### AI-Powered Community Services Navigator for Miami-Dade County

---

**The Problem**: 67% of eligible Miami-Dade residents miss at least one program they qualify for. Language barriers, fragmented directories, and 12+ minute 211 hold times keep vulnerable families from housing, food, healthcare, jobs, and education.

**The Solution**: A free, multilingual AI assistant that connects residents to the right program in under 60 seconds — in English, Spanish, and Haitian Creole.

---

### How It Works

A resident texts or visits the website and asks a question in their language:

> *"Necesito ayuda con el alquiler"*
> *"Mwen bezwen jwenn manje pou fanmi mwen"*
> *"I lost my job and need help"*

The AI identifies their need, searches 37+ verified programs, checks eligibility, and responds with the program name, phone number, address, and next steps — in their language.
